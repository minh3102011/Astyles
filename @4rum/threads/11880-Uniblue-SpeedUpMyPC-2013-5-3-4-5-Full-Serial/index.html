Furthermore SpeedUpMyPC combines all of these features with easy-to-use controls and intelligent problem-prevention technology, to deliver a simple but effective package to help accelerate your computer's performance, improve privacy and tighten security. Now 32 and 64-bit, XP, Vista and Windows compatible and available in 12 languages: English, French, German, Italian, Spanish, Dutch, Portuguese, Danish, Swedish, Norwegian, Russian, and Japanese.

    The Speed tools on offer in SpeedUpMyPC are features that increase the productivity and responsiveness on your PC:

    • The 'CPU Manager' speed tool balances CPU usage to ensure that your tasks get a good share of system resources and thus improves the responsiveness of running applications;
    • The 'Software Launch Booster' speed tool improves the launch of software by boosting allocated resources so that programs start up faster;
    • The 'Start Menu Software Accelerator' speed tool is a predictive feature that improves the launch of software accessed through the Start Menu;
